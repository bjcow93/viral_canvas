Viral Canvas

This is a project written in JavaScript using PaperJS. The goal is to simulate how quickly a virus spreads (in this case through direct contact). It is intended to act as a sandbox simulation, so mess around with the settings, and remember to click the restart button to apply your changes!


Limitations on Settings

  • Width: 100-1300
  • Height: 100-700
  • Num Circles: 1-2500
  • Radius: 1-20
  • Speed: 1-200
  • End Circles: 1-Num Circles
